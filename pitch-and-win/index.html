Winner spotlight: J-Pop

It wins on the exact thing the competition asked for.

Most money

Insurers are a motivated business buyer with a clear return: fewer disputed and fraudulent claims. Disaster agencies, government and telecoms are further routes.

Profitable

Near-zero cost to reach a customer through WhatsApp, plus software margins, over a market that runs the length of the hurricane belt and beyond.

And it protects people

It shields the most vulnerable while it earns. Top money and top impact together is rare, and it is why J-Pop took the top spot.

Next step for the team: one insurer letter of intent and a twenty-household pilot in a single parish before the next storm season.
